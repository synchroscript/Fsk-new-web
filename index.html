About School

We are the most innovative international school in Kosovo based on Finnish education system and utilizing its best practices. The Finnish education system is the best in the world for more than 20 years now and we are very proud of offering 21st century education focusing on the whole child and a student centered learning environment. We are also an accredited Cambridge International School, the only school in Kosovo to offer an internationally recognized qualification. In addition, we are licensed by the Ministry of Education in Kosovo and our certificates and diplomas are recognized all around EU and worldwide.
